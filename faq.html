FAQ

What are you offering?

El Panada Baliwag offers 5 distinct types of empanada that are truly something special! You can choose between the classic, cheesy, spicy, double egg & all meat.

Where are you located?

Our main branch is located at Baliwag, Bulacan. Second branch is at San Rafael, Bulacan (Maguinao Food Park). We also have pop-up stores in Bustos and Pulilan. Sometimes, we have surprise pop-up stores in Malolos, San Fernando, Pampanga & Plaridel, Bulacan

How much is your products?

Our empanadas ranges from 60 pesos to 90 pesos only

When is your business hours?

We are open every Monday to Sunday, 9AM to 8PM

Do you deliver?

Yes, we have partner delivery riders and agencies such as: ordermo, i-Delivery, bilimoko, Go Bayong, Kuya Rider & many more! We can also book a private local rider for you

Are you open for franchising?

As of now: NO. But we are open for business partnership to all investors and aspiring entrepreneurs. Message us to know more.
